FAQ[]

Q: Can the L1Z1X use Harrow when they're defending during ground combat?

A: No, only the active player can use BOMBARDMENT.


Q: When using the hero ability, can fighters and infantry be transported?

A: Yes. During movement abilities (L1Z1X Hero, Argent Hero, Mahact Hero), you may transport units out of systems that contain your tokens.
